Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll a new employee?

Navigate to the Employees section from the sidebar, click Add Employee, fill in the required details, and then use Enroll Face to capture their facial data. Ensure good lighting and multiple angles for best results.

How does the Kiosk Mode work?

Kiosk Mode allows employees to mark attendance using facial recognition without logging in. Simply start the camera, position your face in the frame, and the system will automatically recognize and mark your attendance. The green box indicates successful face detection.

Where can I view attendance reports?

Go to Reports in the sidebar menu. You can filter by date range, employee, department, and download reports in CSV or PDF format. The dashboard also shows real-time attendance statistics.

Is my facial data secure?

Yes! All facial data is encrypted and stored locally on your server. We use advanced anti-spoofing (liveness detection) to prevent fake faces. Your biometric data never leaves your infrastructure.

What if face recognition fails?

Ensure proper lighting, remove glasses/masks if possible, and position your face straight to the camera. The system shows distance and lighting indicators. If issues persist, contact your admin to re-enroll your face data with better quality captures.

How do I configure system settings?

Admins can access Settings from the sidebar to configure working hours, holidays, recognition thresholds, email notifications, camera settings, and more. Changes take effect immediately.

Quick Tips

Good Lighting

Ensure your face is well-lit. Avoid backlighting or shadows for accurate recognition.

Face Camera Directly

Look straight at the camera. Avoid extreme angles or tilting your head.

Correct Distance

Stay 1-2 feet from the camera. Follow the on-screen distance indicators.
