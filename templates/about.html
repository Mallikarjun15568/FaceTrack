About FaceTrack Pro

A next-generation AI-powered attendance system designed to bring accuracy, security and automation to modern workplaces.

What is FaceTrack Pro?

FaceTrack Pro is an AI-powered facial recognition attendance system built using advanced deep learning and InsightFace technology. It eliminates the need for manual registers, RFID cards, or biometric touch sensors.

Our system captures faces in real-time, matches them with high accuracy, and automatically logs attendance with timestamps, snapshots, reports, and analytics — all in a user-friendly dashboard.
